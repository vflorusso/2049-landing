Save Energopolis leveraging nature forces aid!

2049 - The Flip is a game where you need to save Energopolis from fossil energies present danger, with nature forces by your side as allies

Weather events will help you win!

Weather events are key to enhance the attack and defense skills of the magic nature creatures you will pick

Game ambience in between fantasy and reality!

2049 - The Flip takes place in a magical setup, enriched by real ambience components

Take back the city

The battle for Energopolis takes place on the game board where you need to take back 5 places; each of them has its energy "impact". The higher the energy impact of a place, the more Smogbots you will have to defeat

Geodroids will help you conquer the city

Geodroids are by your side to save Energopolis and bring it to "Net zero" through epic fantasy battles.

Pick your class and energy affiliation and become Energopolis' Hero!

In the base version of the game you will have 13 Troopers, 3 Commanders, 1 Mastermind and 1 Warlord.

In order to win, you will need to use your troops with the best possible strategy.

Weather events aid you and your Geodroids

Use both Green Energy tokens and Weather Event cards as they are assigned to you in each season to help Geodroids in battle.

You will be given a number of Green Energy tokens each season and you will need to use them as follows:

  • Line up Geodroids in battle
  • Add a Weather Event to the battlefield
  • Convert the attacked place to Green Energy

Each and every Green Energy token counts!
