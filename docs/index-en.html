Save Energopolis using the elemental forces of nature!

2049 – The Flip is a game where you must save Energopolis from the dangers of fossil energy, with the forces of nature as your allies

Weather events will help you win!

Weather events enhance the attack and defense abilities of the magical nature creatures you choose

A game world that blends fantasy and reality!

2049 – The Flip takes place in a magical setting enriched with real-world environmental elements

In our project, any town can be willing to candidate to "become" Energopolis! Check out what we did for the pioneering municipality of Cetona here (Italian language blog post).

Take back the city

The battle for Energopolis unfolds on the game board, where you must reclaim five key locations, each with its own energy "impact". The higher the energy impact of a place, the more Smogbots you will have to defeat

Geodroids will help you conquer the city

Geodroids are by your side to save Energopolis and lead it to "Net zero" through epic fantasy battles.

Pick your class and energy affinity and become the Hero of Energopolis!

In the base game you command 13 Troopers, 3 Commanders, 1 Mastermind and 1 Warlord.

In order to win, you will need to use your troops with the best possible strategy.

Weather events aid you and your Geodroids

Use both Green Energy tokens and Weather Event cards as they are assigned to you in each season to help Geodroids in battle.

Each season, you receive a set of Green Energy tokens to use strategically:

  • Line up Geodroids in battle
  • Add a Weather Event to the battlefield
  • Convert the attacked place to Green Energy

Each and every Green Energy token counts!
